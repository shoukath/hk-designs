Gigglezz Daycare

Branding Project

We were approached by Gigglezz - A daycare center established in Bangalore to come up with a unique design solution to encapsulate all of the values that Gigglezz stands for.

Started with a vision to provide a one of a kind day care experience with features like CCTV cameras, mobile app to monitor your children's activity, healthy snack options and transportation, all at an affordable price, we were given a challenge to translate the uniqueness of Gigglezz in their branding.

We started from scratch by experimenting with different logo designs, color schemes, design layouts and fonts, offering a wide range of options to our client to pick from.

With a few iterations and tweaks, we decided to go with a visually pleasing color combination that reprsesents the playful and cheerful nature of a daycare center.

This unique mixture ensured that 'Gigglezz' as a brand would stand out from it's competition.
